Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses, tutors, and services.

1. How can I find a tutor?

You can find a tutor by visiting our “Find a Tutor” page, selecting the subject, and contacting the tutor directly through our platform.

2. How can I become a tutor?

To become a tutor, go to the “Become a Tutor” page, fill out the registration form, and submit your credentials. Our team will review and approve your application.

3. What courses are available?

We offer Web Development, Graphic Designing (Canva), Python, MS Office, Spoken Languages (English, Urdu, Farsi), and Quran Teaching courses.

4. How do I register for a course?

Simply create an account using our registration page, select your desired course, and complete the enrollment process.

5. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept online bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and other secure payment gateways. Payment details are provided during course enrollment.
